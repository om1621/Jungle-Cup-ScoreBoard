Diversity
Hack In The North has always been pushing the envelope on making the developer environment a more inclusive one, especially for women developers. The last edition of Hack in The North saw Diversity sponsors and partners such as GitLab and Women Who Code. Further, there were special slots for all-girls teams and special prizes for best women team. This spirit of inclusivity, especially after the abolition of section 377, is going to see a lot of growth in the next editions of Hack In The North. Stay tuned for more updates on our diversity initiatives!
